Maine's Location

Maine is a state in the United States that is located at the top right corner of the U.S. It is a temperate state with hot summers and cold, snowy winters. Maine is the most nothern state in all of America, so it is the coldest. Here are some things you can do in Maine...

  1. Ice Skating in the winter months
  2. Visit Acadia National Park in Bar Harbour
  3. Swim in the rocky beaches along Mount Desert Island
  4. Kayak in the clear waters of Serento
  5. Link to Acadia National Park's official website
